Recent Event

Earthquake in Turkey

Earthquake in Turkey in 2023, on February 6, a magnitude 7.8 earthquake struck Turkey and Syria, leaving tens of thousands dead and widespread damage. Two weeks later, a magnitude 6.3 quake struck Turkey's Hatay province. As of March 6, 2020, hundreds of thousands of people were still in need of adequate shelter and sanitation, and only 10% of the $1 billion requested to help survivors had been collected. The February 6 earthquake was the worst disaster in Turkey's modern history.
